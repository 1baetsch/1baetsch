# QRX - Logic puzzle pApp
QRX is a ["Picross/Nonogram"](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Nonogram) logic puzzle game where you complete QR codes.

## How to play
See this [tutorial](http://www.puzzlemuseum.com/griddler/gridins.htm).
